Panini Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 5 Panini Breads
  • 500 g Chicken Boneless
  • 150 g Mozzarella Cheese
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • tbsp Mayonnaise
  • tbsp Tomato Ketchup

    • Marination

    • 1 tsp Salt
    • 1 tsp Black Pepper
    • 1 tsp Paprika
    • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
    • 1 tsp Garlic Paste
    • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
    • 1 tsp Chilli Sauce

    Instruction:

    1. Cut up the boneless chicken into small bite-sized chunks then add into a bowl along with the ingredients for marination
    2. In a pan heat up 1 tbsp oil on medium heat until hot then add the marinated chicken mixture
    3. Cook for 6-8 minutes until the chicken has changed colour
    4. As the chicken is cooking finely dice the onion and bell pepper then add into the pan and cook for a further 4-5 minutes
    5. Finely dice the tomato then add into the pan along with the tomato puree and cook for another 2-3 minutes
    6. Once the chicken mixture is cooked slice the panini breads lengthwise then add the tomato ketchup and mayonnaise
    7. Add the chicken mixture to the panini along with mozzarella cheese.
    8. In a grill pan add the oil then brush evenly over the pan and allow to heat up before adding the panini then grill for 2-3 minutes on both side until lightly charred.
    9. Serve with a side of French fries and enjoy!
